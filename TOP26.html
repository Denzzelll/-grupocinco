Country: Greece

Grilled lamb chops are a traditional Greek dish that is popular throughout the country and usually enjoyed as the main course.

Lamb chops are usually marinated in various combinations of olive oil, lemon juice, and a variety of fresh herbs before they are grilled on traditional charcoal barbecues.

They are commonly paired with potatoes, salads, or the creamy and refreshing tzatziki sauce.
